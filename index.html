Therma Cameras And How It Functions

Thermal cameras, also known as infrared cameras, detect temperature differences in objects or environments, capturing images based on heat radiation.

How it works;

  • Infrared sensors detect temperature differences.
  • Thermal energy is converted into electrical signals.
  • Signals are processed into images.
  • Surveillance (security, search and rescue)
  • Predictive maintenance (industrial, electrical)
  • Building inspection (energy efficiency, moisture detection)
  • Medical imaging (diagnosing injuries, cancer detection)
  • Wildlife observation (research, conservation)
  • Firefighting (locating hotspots)
  • HVAC inspection (heating, ventilation, air conditioning)
  • Industrial process monitoring
